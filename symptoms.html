Symptoms of Breast Cancer

Common signs and symptoms of breast cancer may include:

  • A lump in the breast or underarm
  • Changes in the size, shape, or appearance of the breast
  • Unexplained pain in the breast
  • Skin changes, such as redness or dimpling
  • Nipple discharge other than breast milk

If you notice any of these symptoms, it is important to seek medical advice for early detection and diagnosis.
