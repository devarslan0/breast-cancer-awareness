Prevention

While breast cancer cannot always be prevented, certain lifestyle choices and precautions can help lower the risk. This includes regular exercise, maintaining a healthy weight, limiting alcohol consumption, and getting regular screenings.

Key prevention tips include:

  • Healthy Diet: Eating a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains can help reduce cancer risk.
  • Physical Activity: Regular exercise can help manage weight and hormone levels, both of which influence breast cancer risk.
  • Limiting Alcohol: Reducing alcohol consumption can lower your risk of breast cancer, as alcohol can increase estrogen levels.
  • Breastfeeding: Breastfeeding for several months may lower the risk of breast cancer.
  • Regular Screening: Early detection through mammograms and self-exams is key to improving outcomes.
