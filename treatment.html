Treatment Options

Treatment for breast cancer depends on the stage and type of cancer, and may include the following options:

  • Surgery to remove the tumor or the whole breast (mastectomy)
  • Chemotherapy to kill cancer cells or shrink the tumor
  • Radiation therapy to target remaining cancer cells
